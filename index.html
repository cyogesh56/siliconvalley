Silicon Valley Trip

Class of 2019

Overview

In August of 2019, a group of 4 students from DePaul University traveled to Silicon Valley to research innovation. During our research trip, we visited  eight companies and met with various people, ranging from product professionals to CEOs and founders to discuss their approach towards innovation. During the meeting  we discussed where innovative ideas come from to examples of innovative ideas or products .

Innovation Analysis Framework

This section provides a high level understanding of our analysis methods and research questions.

  • Inspiration

    Where do innovative ideas come form in your organization? How are internal employees involved in the innovation process? What is the role of the external stakeholders such as customers, competitiors, vendors, etc.

  • Idea incubation process

    Who can be involved in the innovation process? What are the enables and inhibitors? Where does the idea incubation process start?

  • Focal Innovation and its impact

    An example of recent innovative products or service. Trigger for the development of product/services. Understanding the extent of innovation impact.
